The Journey

From investigations to infrastructure

A career built on pattern recognition, evidence integrity, and making sound decisions under pressure — whether the stakes involve people or systems.

2010 – 2016
International Law Enforcement

Six years of cross-border investigations with Interpol and Europol. Learned evidence integrity, precise documentation under regulatory standards, and the discipline of making decisions with limited information under high stakes.

2019 – 2021
Global Incident Management

Led major incident response for enterprise clients at DXC Technology — coordinating cross-functional teams across global time zones. Discovered that the skills from investigations translated directly: triage, evidence, structured communication.

2021 – 2026
